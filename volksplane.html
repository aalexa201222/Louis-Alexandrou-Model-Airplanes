VOLKSPLANE VP-1..


Volksplane Design

Scale 1: 3 design based on the three view drawing and a plan with construction details, of the real VP-1. It was constructed mainly from 4mm ordinary plywood, balsa wood and white polystyrene.

The wings are mounted on the body and held with handmade aluminum airfoil-shaped struts. Weights were added to all moving surfaces, for easy movement and flutter prevention. Scale all moveable single piece tail surfaces. The landing gear with shock absorber tension springs was made from 4mm aluminum and the cowl from balsa wood

The 48cc engine was taken from a weed- whacker and was converted for this purpose. The handmade RC pilot made from white polystyrene moves head and hand.
Wingspan: 2,54m
Wing Area: 1689 sq.in.
Weight: 8,5 kg
Wing loading: 25,6 oz/sq.ft
