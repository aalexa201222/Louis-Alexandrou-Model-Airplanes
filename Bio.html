LOUIS ALEXANDROU

Airplane modeller

Hi, i am Louis Alexandrou.
I was born in a village in Nicosia called Meniko. I studied electrical engineer at Higher Technical Institute of Cyprus.
At a young age, I was interested in building things using materials that I could find in my everyday life. Although, I was most interested in building airplanes. At the age of 15 I bult my first airplane and now, I made more that 40 airplanes from scratch. I also built some RC boats and cars by wood and toys. I used to make my airplanes by useless materials like pipes, milk containers and wood cutting engines. Now, most of my airplanes are electric and made out of wood.
