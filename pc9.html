PILATUS PC-9..


The military turboprop trainer of the Cyprus Air Force.‚Äù The model was designed in 1994 in 1:5 scale, based on the dimensions I got from the three sides of the drawing and a small mockup of the real PC-9. It was made mainly from ordinary 4mm plywood, white polystyrene and palsa wood. The machine cover was made from 0.25mm printing press aluminum and the canopy from plastic milk bottles

In 2014, improvements were made to the original model and it was converted to electric. Hacker brushless motor with Ramoser four blade variable pitch propeller, Jeti spin Esc 99A and 8Series 29.6V Lipo batteries were used. The mechanical retracts were replaced with new electrical ones and an additional piece of flaps was placed under the body.
