FLAMINGO..


It was made mainly of aluminum, plywood and polystyrene. All handmade including propeller, wheel bases and spinner. Body from 0.5mm aluminum sheet. Tail, ailerons and flaps from 0.25mm aluminum sheet. A used 38cc lawnmower converted for this purpose was used.

Wing Area: 1825 sq. in.
Weight: 11 kg
Wing Loading: 53oz/sq.ft.
