Astrapi..

After a lot of studying, the drawings of my new model have been completed. Its name is Astrapi and it has been specially designed for slotted flaps. Well developed slotted flaps, at 40 degrees offer the highest additional lift and have the lowest drag, compared to split or plain flaps, so they can also be used half extended for shorter and slower takeoffs. These advantages of slotted flaps make it possible to build a fast model, strong and rugged with higher than usual wing loading. With slotted flaps down, it can land at trainer speeds.


The flaps calculations are based on Andy Lennon's Articles on slotted flaps on airplane models, as well as on naca reports 808 and 664. Andy successfully developed the flaps in airplane models with simple rectangular constant cord wings. I will try to build these flaps in a more efficient tapered wing (taper ratio 0,5).Chord is varied across the span. The same happens with the flap. Calculations for each flap support will differ. It is a difficult task, but it is quite interesting.
