Saini Design

A low cost model, mainly constructed from white polystyrene. Also used light ply, for firewall section and sections for mounting undercarriage and wings. A small quantity of balsa wood was also used. The aim was a very lightly loaded, yet solid construction. Wingspan: 135cm, Length 133 cm, Wing area: 766sq.in, Weight: 1282gr Wing loading (with 3S 2600mAh lipo): 8,5 oz/sq.ft
